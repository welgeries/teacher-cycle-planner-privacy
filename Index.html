Privacy Policy for Teacher Cycle Planner

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Teacher Cycle Planner is designed to help teachers manage rotating cycle schedules, classes, duties, and reminders directly on their device.

Information We Collect

Teacher Cycle Planner does not collect personal data from users through a remote server in the current standalone version of the app.

How Your Data Is Used

Schedule data that you import or enter manually, such as cycle calendars, classes, room numbers, and supervision duties, is stored locally on your device so the app can display your schedule and create local reminders.

Files You Import

If you import CSV or PDF files, those files are processed on-device to help extract schedule information. In the current standalone version, imported files are not uploaded to a company server for processing.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses local notifications on your device to notify you before classes or duties. These notifications are scheduled and stored by iOS on your device.

Data Sharing

Teacher Cycle Planner does not sell, rent, or share your schedule data with third parties in the current standalone version of the app.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you edit it, delete the app, or clear the app’s data through device actions such as uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for teachers and school staff. It is not directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If the app later adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or other online services, this policy should be updated before release.
